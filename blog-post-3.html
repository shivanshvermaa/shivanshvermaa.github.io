People and Dress Colour Identification using YOLOv3 and K-Means Clustering

By Shivansh Verma

Often in a picture or in Live feeds people want to identify a particular person who is wearing something distinguishing like a redshirt or a bluepant.

In this project I used YOLOv3 to detect people in a frame and used their generated bounding boxes to extract each individual person. This helped me to remove individuals and the area of the frame they were part of for further processing.

Post which I used K-Means Clustering technique to find the majority colours that are present in that screen. These values would then be used to create a colour histogram that helped to understand what the dress colour of the person in question is.

TL;DR

• Designed an object-detection model to identify a person and their dress color using TensorFlow and OpenCV.
• Used YOLO object-detection model to a person and color recognition using K-Nearest Neighbors algorithm.
• Aids Law enforcement to identify a Person of Interest faster in crowded area and use facial recognition to confirm.
